About Outback Gems & Minerals

Welcome to Outback Gems and Minerals. We're a couple who share a passion for faceting, fossicking, and all things lapidary.

Like many enthusiasts, we found it challenging to source quality materials at affordable prices. So we began travelling to various locations, building relationships with local miners and collectors to find better options.

Along our travels, we made valuable contacts and collected materials firsthand. From the opal fields of Yowah to the sapphire mines of Queensland, we've explored diverse geological locations to bring you authentic materials at reasonable prices.

What drives us is providing fellow enthusiasts - whether you're into faceting, fossicking, or collecting - with access to quality materials at fair prices. We understand the importance of having good materials for your projects without breaking the bank.
